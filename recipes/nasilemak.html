Nasi Lemak

Description

What is Nasi Lemak? It is a Malaysian national dish mainly consisting of rice, egg, sambal and cucumber. Optionally, fried chicken and peanuts are also common in the dish. Although it is Malaysia's dish, it is also served in neighbouring countries Singapore, Indonesia and Thailand.

Ingredients

  • Coconut milk
  • Jasmine rice
  • Fried chicken (optional)
  • Eggs
  • Cucumber
  • Sambal

Steps

  1. Cook the rice with coconut milk.
  2. Boil or fry the eggs.
  3. Cut the cucumbers
  4. Make the sambal
  5. Fry the chicken (optional)
  6. Assemble the ingredients together. Rice should have sambal on top or the side. Eggs, Cucumber or chicken must be at the side of the rice. Serve on pandan leaf like how Malays would do it.
