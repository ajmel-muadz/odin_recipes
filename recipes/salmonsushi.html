Salmon Sushi

Description

What is Salmon Sushi? This is a simple dish from Japan in which salmon is placed on top of glutinous rice wrapped in seaweed. Salmon sushi is simple ingredients-wise, but making it takes a lot of practice to get right. Salmon sushi can be eaten with wasabi if you're a masochist.

Ingredients

  • Seaweed
  • Salmon
  • Glutinous rice
  • Wasabi (optional)

Steps

  1. Get fresh salmon from the store.
  2. Cook glutinous rice.
  3. Wrap seaweed around rice, placing salmon on top of it. You can choose either raw or cooked salmon.
  4. Serve with wasabi if you're not a coward.
